How GCleaner Works

Get started in seconds with these simple steps

1

Launch App

Open GCleaner and grant access to your photo library. The app will load all your photos automatically.

2

Start Swiping

Photos appear one by one. Swipe left to delete unwanted photos, swipe right to keep the good ones.

3

Auto-Advance

After each decision, the next photo automatically appears. No need to tap or navigate manually.

4

Clean Gallery

Enjoy your organized photo library with only the photos you want to keep, freeing up valuable storage space.

Support & FAQ

Get help with GCleaner and find answers to common questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Tap the "Gallery" button in the top left corner of the main screen. This will show all your photos in a familiar iPhone gallery layout, similar to the native Photos app.

Swiping left shows a red "Delete" label and permanently removes the photo from both GCleaner and your iPhone's photo library. Swiping right shows a green "Keep" label and preserves the photo in both locations.

Tap the "Options" button in the top right corner of the main screen. This opens a settings screen where you can choose between Turkish and English. All app text will update to your selected language.

Currently, GCleaner performs permanent deletions to help you quickly clean up your photo library. Make sure you want to delete a photo before swiping left. We recommend backing up important photos before using the app.

Use iPhone's standard back gesture - swipe from the left edge of the screen to the right. This will return you to the main photo sorting screen from any other view.

GCleaner is designed for modern iOS devices and requires iOS 14.0 or later. The app works best on iPhone models that support the standard back gesture navigation.
